03

Rules to know before you go

✓Cans only in coolers — no glass bottles allowed anywhere on festival grounds.
✓No private campfires. One large community fire is tended by Fire Watch volunteers; small grills are fine for cooking.
✓No electricity at campsites — plan for battery or solar power.
✓Pets must stay leashed and out of stage and crowded areas.
✓Wristbands go on the right wrist for the whole weekend. Kids get name tags with parent contact and medical info.
✓No stereos, amps, or radios at the campsite during the festival.
✓Cell service generally works on-island; EMS/Fire keeps an emergency radio on-site.
✓Hideaway Trail runs one-way during the festival — mind the posted traffic patterns.
